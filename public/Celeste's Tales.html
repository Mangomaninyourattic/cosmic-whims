A Love Letter to the Silver Screen

  1. Spirited Away (2001)

      Directed by Hayao Miyazaki, this film follows a young girl's journey in a mysterious and magical world.

  2. Princess Mononoke (1997)

      An epic tale of a young prince's quest to reconcile the natural and human worlds while battling powerful forces.

  3. Howl's Moving Castle (2004)

      This film tells the story of a young woman cursed into an elderly form and her adventures with a magical castle.

  4. Kiki's Delivery Service (1989)

      Follow the journey of a young witch as she starts her own delivery service in a charming seaside town.

  5. Laputa: Castle in the Sky (1986)

      An adventure involving a boy and a girl who seek the legendary floating city of Laputa.

  6. Ponyo (2008)

      A whimsical story of a fish who wants to become a human and her friendship with a young boy.

  7. Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind (1984)

      This film is set in a post-apocalyptic world and follows a young princess trying to protect her people from toxic spores and giant insects.

  8. The Wind Rises (2013)

      A fictionalized biography of Jiro Horikoshi, the designer of Japan's World War II fighter planes.

  9. Castle in the Sky

      This is the story of a young boy and girl searching for the mythical floating castle of Laputa while pursued by pirates and government agents.

  10. Porco Rosso (1992)

      Follow the adventures of a World War I pilot turned pig as he battles air pirates in the Adriatic Sea.

  11. When Marnie Was There (2014)

      A touching story about a lonely girl who forms a special bond with a mysterious girl named Marnie.

  12. The Cat Returns (2002)

      A spin-off of "Whisper of the Heart," it follows a girl who becomes involved with a cat kingdom after saving a cat.

  13. Grave of the Fireflies (1988)

      A heart-wrenching tale of two siblings struggling to survive during World War II.

  14. Barbie in the Nutcracker (2001)

      Barbie stars as Clara in this adaptation of the classic Nutcracker ballet, where she embarks on a magical journey to help a prince break an enchantment.

  15. Barbie as Rapunzel (2002)

      In this film, Barbie takes on the role of Rapunzel, a creative and artistic young woman locked in a tower, who uses her talents to escape and find her true love.

  16. Barbie of Swan Lake (2003)

      Barbie plays Odette, a girl who is transformed into a swan by an evil sorcerer's spell. She must find a way to break the curse and save a magical forest.

  17. Barbie as the Princess and the Pauper (2004)

      Barbie takes on the dual roles of Princess Anneliese and pauper Erika in this story of friendship, love, and adventure.

  18. Barbie Fairytopia (2005)

      Barbie stars as Elina, a fairy who embarks on a journey to save her fairyland from darkness by finding a magical guardian.

  19. Barbie and the Magic of Pegasus (2005)

      Barbie plays Princess Annika in this fairy tale where she must rescue her kingdom from an evil wizard with the help of a magical winged horse.

  20. Barbie Fairytopia: Mermaidia (2006)

      Barbie returns as Elina in this sequelto Fairytopia, where she ventures underwater to save her friend Nalu and Mermaidia from danger.

  21. The Barbie Diaries (2006)

      Barbie stars as a teenager named Barbie Roberts who navigates the ups and downs of high school, friendship, and secrets in this modern-day story.

  22. Barbie in the 12 Dancing Princesses (2006)

      Barbie takes on the role of Princess Genevieve, one of twelve sisters who discover a magical world where they can dance freely. They must work together to save their kingdom.

  23. Barbie as the Island Princess (2007)

      Barbie portrays Rosella, a shipwrecked girl who grows up on an island with animal friends. She must learn about her royal heritage and return to her kingdom.

  24. Barbie and the Diamond Castle (2008)

      Barbie and her friend Teresa find a magical world inside a diamond castle and go on an adventure to save it from an evil muse.

  25. Barbie in a Christmas Carol (2008)

      Barbie stars as Eden Starling, a selfish singer who is visited by three spirits on Christmas Eve, leading to a change of heart.

  26. Barbie Presents: Thumbelina (2009)

      Barbie plays the role of Thumbelina, a tiny girl who embarks on a journey to save her home from being destroyed by construction.

  27. Barbie and the Three Musketeers (2009)

      Barbie takes on the role of Corinne, a young woman who dreams of becoming a musketeer and works to protect the French throne from an evil plot.

  28. Barbie in a Mermaid Tale (2010)

      Barbie portrays Merliah Summers, a surfer girl who discovers her mermaid heritage and must save her underwater kingdom.

  29. Barbie: A Fashion Fairytale (2010)

      Barbie stars as herself in a fashion-themed adventure, where she helps save a fashion house from closing down.

  30. Barbie: Princess Charm School (2011)

      Barbie plays Blair Willows, a girl who discovers she is a princess and attends Princess Charm School to learn royal etiquette.

  31. Barbie in a Mermaid Tale 2 (2012)

      Merliah Summers returns for another underwater adventure, this time to save her mermaid kingdom from a threat.
